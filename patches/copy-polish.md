# Copy Polish — Review Summary

## Fixes Applied

### 1. Philosophical Misattribution (CRITICAL)
**"The Incoherence of the Philosophers"** in The Philosophical Warrior reading list was attributed to **Ibn Rushd (Averroes)** — this is wrong. Al-Ghazali wrote *The Incoherence of the Philosophers* (Tahāfut al-Falāsifa, 1095). Ibn Rushd wrote *The Incoherence of the Incoherence* (Tahāfut al-Tahāfut, 1180) as a rebuttal. Fixed title to the correct Ibn Rushd work and updated description to clarify the relationship.

### 2. Wrong Book Title (The Existential Pragmatist readings)
**"The World Beyond Your Head"** by Matthew Crawford was described as "Motorcycle repair as existential practice" — that's Crawford's other book, *Shop Class as Soulcraft* (2009). *The World Beyond Your Head* (2015) is about attention and cognitive engagement. Fixed title to match the description.

### 3. Wiredu Book Title
**"Philosophy and an African Intellectual Culture"** → **"Philosophy and an African Culture"** (1980). The word "Intellectual" isn't in the title.

### 4. Gandhi Book Title
**"The Hind Swaraj"** → **"Hind Swaraj"**. No article in the original title.

### 5. Examined Path Scenario Count
Card said **"15 scenarios"** but the path delivers 16 (15 EXAMINED_IDS + The Last Question). Essential (9+1=10) and Deep Cut (23+1=24) both count The Last Question correctly. Fixed to "16 scenarios" and "Sixteen carefully chosen dilemmas."
